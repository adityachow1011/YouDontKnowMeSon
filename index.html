Tips to remember


  • - Focus on both cardio as well as weight training.
  • - Cardio time must be little bit greater than weight training time.

Bulking & weight gain

Tips to remember


  • - 8 to 12 rep for 4 sets till muscle failure
  • - rest time - near to 2mins between each sets
  • - focus on warmup exercise to make the body ready for heavy weights(dynamic streaching).
  • - focus on warm up sets(4-6 reps) before doing actual sets.
  • - Try to increase load in every set without changing the rest time duration.
  • - Posing between sets to increase the training intensity and to establish mind & muscle connection.
  • - Take sip of water after every sets(BCAA or glucose) to keep the body hydrated.
  • - Cool down streaching exercise for muscle flexibility after whole workout
  • - After training take protein to get amino acids asap.
